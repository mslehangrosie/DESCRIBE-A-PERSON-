🎯 IELTS Speaking Part 2 - Topic: DESCRIBE A PERSON (Interactive System) 🎯

💡 Tiêu Chí Chuẩn Bài Nói Part 2 (150 từ ~ 1.5 - 2 phút)

1. Intro: Paraphrase đề bài (1 câu) ➜ 2. Body: Trả lời lần lượt các ý Cue Card (mỗi ý từ 2-3 câu dài) ➜ 3. Conclusion: Nêu cảm nghĩ tổng kết (1 câu)

🎯 Các đề thuộc bộ đề có thể áp dụng chung ý tưởng IDOL (Rosé):

  • • Describe a famous person you would like to meet
  • • Describe a person who is good at learning and speaking new languages (English/Korean)
  • • Describe a person who initially wore unusually bold and artistic outfits
Đề mẫu: Describe a famous person you would like to meet (Rosé)
Target Band: 7.0+ | Từ vựng chủ đề tự nhiên
1. Who this person is & How you know them
  • Rosé from BLACKPINK (all-time idol)
  • Talented & internationally famous singer
  • First came across her music online during debut
  • Blown away by her charisma
2. What kind of music/style they have
  • Performs K-pop, blends pop, soft rock, and indie
  • Stands out from many other artists
  • Bold and artistic fashion sense (unusual combinations)
  • Distinctive vocal color (emotional & powerful)
3. Why they are successful (Languages / Background)
  • Born in Australia, moved to South Korea to chase dream
  • Fluent in English and Korean (gifted at languages)
  • Won 1st place in YG Entertainment audition (>300 contestants)
  • Broken global records ("On The Ground", "Gone")
4. Why you want to meet them & Conclusion
  • Inspiring story of hard work and resilience
  • Want to attend her live concert in the future
  • Unquestionably the artist I genuinely admire most

📝 Bài mẫu hoàn chỉnh (Sample Answer):

"Today, I’d like to talk about Rosé from BLACKPINK, a talented and internationally famous singer who has been my all-time idol. I first came across her music online when BLACKPINK debuted, and completely blown away by her charisma.

Rosé mainly performs K-pop, but her music often blends elements of pop, soft rock, and indie, which makes her stand out from many other artists. If my memory serves me right, she was born in Australia and later moved to South Korea to pursue her dream. She is exceptionally gifted at languages, speaking both English and Korean fluently. Before debuting, she competed against more than three hundred contestants in an audition, and she won first place, showing how extraordinary her talent is. Aside from music, she is also famous for wearing unusually bold, artistic outfits that challenge traditional fashion norms.

One of the main reasons I like her so much is her distinctive vocal color, which is both emotional and powerful. She has broken several global records and won numerous awards. If I had the chance in the future, I would definitely attend her concert to experience her music live. She is unquestionably the only singer I genuinely admire."

🎯 Các đề thuộc bộ đề có thể áp dụng chung ý tưởng BESTIE (Hương / Minh / Linh / Hoa):

  • • Describe a friend from your childhood / A good friend who is important to you
  • • Describe a person who solved a problem in a smart way / good at planning
  • • Describe a person who is gifted at singing or has a special talent
  • • Describe a person you initially disliked but eventually became good friends with
  • • Describe a well-dressed person you know
  • • Describe a time when a friend persuaded you to do something
  • • Describe a child who loves drawing/painting (Có thể biến tấu thành Hương lúc nhỏ hoặc cháu của mình)
Đề mẫu: Describe a good friend who is important / solved a problem smartly (Hương)
Target Band: 7.0+ | Ý tưởng linh hoạt, dễ nói
1. Who they are & How you met
  • My closest friend named Huong (same age)
  • Known each other for more than 5 years since high school
  • First met when we became deskmates
  • Gradually grew closer over time
2. Their key personality & Talents
  • Incredibly intelligent and well-dressed
  • Remarkable ability to stay calm under pressure
  • Loves reading books (romance novels)
  • Gifted at singing soft pop/ballads (makes her stand out)
3. A specific story (Solving a problem / Persuading)
  • Got lost in an amusement park last year
  • Instead of panicking, she patiently checked location on phone
  • Found me within minutes (smart problem-solving skills)
  • Persuaded me to join an English contest to step out of my comfort zone
4. Why they are important & Conclusion
  • Always gives me honest and thoughtful advice
  • Her voice and presence always brighten my mood
  • Truly admire her; hope our friendship lasts for many years

📝 Bài mẫu hoàn chỉnh (Sample Answer):

"Today, I'm going to talk about one of the most important friends in my life, Huong. We are the same age and have known each other for more than five years, ever since high school when we first became deskmates and quickly grew close.

What impresses me the most about Huong is that she is incredibly intelligent and gifted at many things, including singing and fashion design. She has a remarkable ability to stay calm in challenging situations and always comes up with practical solutions. I still remember vividly a time when I got lost in a crowded amusement park. Instead of panicking, she calmly checked my location on her phone and managed to find me within minutes. That really showed her smart problem-solving skills. Besides that, she is a well-dressed person who loves art and often helps me style my outfits.

She is someone I truly trust because she always gives me honest and thoughtful advice. Last year, she successfully persuaded me to join a speaking contest to step out of my comfort zone. Overall, I truly admire her intelligence and kindness, and I hope our friendship will last for many more years."

🎯 Các đề thuộc bộ đề có thể áp dụng chung ý tưởng FAMILY/MOM (Mẹ / Dì / Anh chị em):

  • • Describe a person you know who has a successful business / works in a successful company
  • • Describe an older person you enjoy talking to / inspired you to do something interesting
  • • Describe a person who loves to grow plants at home or in the garden (Aunt/Mom)
  • • Describe a person who likes to look after the natural world / often helps others
  • • Describe a person who makes plans a lot and is good at planning
  • • Describe a person you know who would like to choose a career in the medical field
Đề mẫu: Describe an older person you enjoy talking to / has a successful business (Mom)
Target Band: 7.0+ | Cảm xúc, chân thật, từ vựng ghi điểm
1. Who this person is & Their job
  • My mother, an older person in her early forties
  • Runs a small garment/tailor company right at our house
  • Manages more than 30 employees
  • Work is incredibly busy with tight schedules
2. What they do daily (Planning & Plants hobby)
  • Deals with customer orders & unexpected problems
  • Good at planning out production timelines perfectly
  • Loves to grow plants & organic vegetables in the backyard corner
  • Spends an hour watering them to look after the natural world
3. What you talk about & How they inspired you
  • Love hearing her stories about how she handles difficult situations
  • Shares valuable lessons about patience and hard work
  • Inspired me to attend a meaningful course (teaching/medical)
  • Encouraged me when I doubted my own ability
4. Your feelings & Conclusion
  • Incredibly wise, always gives practical advice
  • Broadens my perspective and mindset
  • She is one of my biggest inspirations in life

📝 Bài mẫu hoàn chỉnh (Sample Answer):

"Today, I’m going to talk about my mom, who is an older person with a really interesting life and someone I truly enjoy talking to. She is in her early forties and runs a successful garment company right at our house, where she manages more than thirty employees.

Her daily life is incredibly busy but meaningful. She is someone who makes plans a lot and is expert at scheduling production timelines. Despite the heavy pressure, she always maintains a positive attitude and a strong sense of responsibility. Besides her business, she is also passionate about looking after the natural world, spending time growing organic vegetables and flowers in our small backyard to relax her mind.

Whenever we talk, I love hearing her stories about how she built her business from scratch and handled difficult situations. She recently inspired me to take a new course by pushing me to challenge myself. I enjoy talking to her because she is incredibly wise and always gives me practical advice. Her lessons have broadened my perspective, and she remains one of my biggest inspirations in life."
